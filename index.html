About

The Student Activity Council's Technical Club proudly presents the Bi-Weekly Coding Challenge, a platform designed to ignite the coding passion in every student, regardless of their academic background. Our mission is to foster a vibrant coding community where individuals can embark on a coding journey from scratch. Every fortnight, we host a coding contest to test and enhance your problem-solving abilities and programming skills. To recognize and celebrate exceptional talent, we culminate the year with an offline Ultimate Coding Challenge, bringing together the top performers from each contest.

Vision

Our vision is to foster a community of passionate coders at Aditya College of Engineering and Technology, encouraging continuous practice and innovation.


Mission


Our mission is to provide students with the resources and challenges needed to develop their coding skills, preparing them for successful careers in technology

Advantages

  • Enhanced problem-solving and logic-building skills
  • Improved coding proficiency and familiarity with different programming languages
  • Better preparation for campus placements and technical interviews
  • Opportunities to collaborate and learn from peers
  • Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance
